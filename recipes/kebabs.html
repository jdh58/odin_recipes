Yummy Honey Chicken Kebabs

Description:

This delicious yummy honey chicken kabab takes the best from both the bee family and the bird family, and combines it into one savory dish!

Whip this up at a summertime barbecue and you're sure to please the crowd! Just one warning: get ready to make seconds!

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Bell Peppers
  • Garlic
  • Skewers

Steps:

  1. Make the Marinade
  2. Marinate the Ingredients
  3. Assemble the Kebabs
  4. Grill the Kebabs
